Cooter's Garage

Owned and operated by Cooter Davenport and one of the nicest guys around. Cooter can do everything from change your headlight to building a fast engine for racing and and a low price too. Call around and see. IF you can find a lower price, Cooter can match it. Cooter has his own tow truck as well. Day or night Cooter get you you running and back out ont he road in no time!



Duke Family Farm

The Duke farm supplie the local market with fresh vegetables and eggs. Jesse Duke along with help from his nephews Bo, Luke and sometimes his niece Daisy work hard to provide only the best crops every year. Jesse's squash is the biggest in the state and the tatiest too! Just don't ask for his moonshine recipie, Jesse no longer manufacturers moonshine on the account of his promise to the U.S. government to no longer manufacture his delicious brew. When the Duke's aren't working hard, they are playing hard and can be found at the Boar's Nest most Friday nights.
